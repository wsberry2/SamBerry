Liverpool FC

Liverpool FC is one of the biggest soccer clubs in the world. Liverpool FC competes in England's top league, The Barclays Premier League as well as Europe's top division, the Champion's League. Liverpool is admired for its rich history as a soccer club. Liverpool's home stadium is named Anfield, which holds around 54,000 people. Liverpool is nicknamed The Reds among supporters.

Mohamed Salah

Mohammed Salah is one of the most talented players in the world. He played for Roma and Chelsea prior to joining Liverpool FC. Salah enjoyed his best season in 2017 receiving the Golden Boot and Player of the Season. He help carry Liverpool to its first Champions League final since 2005 before falling to the reigning champion Real Madrid.
